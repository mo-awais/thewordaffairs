About me

Check out my timeline and grow with me!

  • 2020

    My Humble Beginnings

    This podcast began in May 2020, in a time of uncertainty and continues to grow today. Episodes are uploaded every Friday and as a student myself, the discussions of culture, society and religion have resonated with other students and young people. It’s small but consistent and the journey will only continue to take us places, with the aim of being able to talk about matters more openly and freely.

  • Onwards

    After graduating with a degree in biomedicine, I decided to go down the physician associate route - follow me along the journey on Instagram and my podcast as I open up about my experiences as a physician associate student, and balancing my deen alongside my academic commitments.
